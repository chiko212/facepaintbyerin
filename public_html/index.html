Face Painting Artist & Entertainer

You will be amazed! I'm a fully insured professional entertainer offering a unique style of face painting with a background in art and design. I've honed my face painting skills over the past 14 years working evenings, weekends and holidays across a variety of events in the Halton, Hamilton & Niagara regions.

And when it comes to parties - I bring it all. A professional custom-built setup, a friendly and patient disposition, and the creative know-how to paint anything your mind can imagine from animals, super heroes, cartoon characters, team logos and more.

From small birthday parties to large corporate events, you'll be amazed when you choose Face Paint by Erin to entertain your guests!

Service Range & Fees

I offer face painting services to events in the Halton, Hamilton and Mississauga regions in Ontario, Canada at a rate of $125/hour with a minimum 2 hour booking. This includes travel to the event location, setup 15 minutes before the scheduled start time and clean-up after. Additional travel fees may apply to events outside this range.

1 hour bookings are available for events exclusively in Oakville at a rate of $150.

Additional artists can be sub-contracted at a cost of $20/artist plus their hourly rate.
