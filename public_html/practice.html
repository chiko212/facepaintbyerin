Code of Practice

We only use professional face and body paint, cosmetic glitter and products that are skin safe and comply with EUA/FDA regulations. All of our paints are water based and easily removed with mild soap and water.

For the safety of all clients, we follow some basic guidelines during events:

  1. all paints, brushes, sponges and water are kept as clean as possible
  2. it is not recommended that children under two years of age be painted
  3. for children ages two to three years, we recommend a small design on the cheek, hand or arm only
  4. children/adults who are obviously unwell should not be painted (runny nose, cold sore, eczema, open wounds, etc)
  5. we will not paint anyone against their wishes, we stop painting if they become uncomfortable or change their mind

Artist's Preferences

The artist reserves the right to work in a safe and comfortable environment. We supply all the equipment required to safely paint your guests faces. With advance notice, we can also supply a table and two chairs at no extra charge.

When preparing an area for us at your event, we ask that you consider the following:

  1. for all events, an area away from loud noises to make communication with guests easier
  2. for all events, a well-lit area and access to water is preferable
  3. for outdoor events, a tent or covered area to protect from the sun and rain
  4. when supplying table and chairs, we ask that they are sturdy and an appropriate/regular height
