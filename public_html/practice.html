Code of Practice

  1. children/adults who are obviously unwell will not be painted (runny nose, cold sore, open wounds, eye infections or any visible skin condition)
  2. it is not recommended that children under two years of age be painted

Removal of Face Paint

It is recommended that clients remove face paint with mild soap and water.


Artist's Preferences

The artist reserves the right to work in a safe and comfortable environment. When preparing an area for us at your event, we ask that you consider the following:

  1. for outdoor events, a tent or covered area to protect from the sun and rain
  2. for all events, an area away from loud noises like DJs, bands or speakers
  3. when providing a table, please ensure it's large enough to accommodate our supplies
  4. when providing chairs, please ensure they are sturdy and an appropriate size
