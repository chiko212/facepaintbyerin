Meet Erin:

A highly creative, strategically-minded and results-focused design professional. As a graduate of Niagara College, she specializes in graphic design production. A life long artist, Erin has applied her education and experiences to her developing career as a professional face painter. With over 5 years of experience working with clients, Erin is very friendly and personable. Some of her favourite projects to work on are:

  • Face painting & body art
  • Visual merchandising
  • Branding, identity & logo design
  • Marketing print & layout design
  • Illustration & paint design

If you are looking for a professional face painter or consultation on a design project, please email any questions about availability, booking requests or estimates.
