Meet your Artist

My name is Erin!

I'm a life-long artist who loves to draw and paint. I started my face painting career in high school by painting faces at school fun fairs and birthday parties.

Since then I've gained over 14 years of experience by entertaining guests at 350+ corporate, public and private events; bringing strong interpersonal skills and providing top-notch service your guests are sure to remember.

With my background in graphic design, I offer a wide variety of customizable face paint designs and can quickly adapt to suit the needs of your event.
