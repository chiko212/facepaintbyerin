Meet Erin

A life long artist, Erin has studied traditional art techniques and graphic design production at Niagara College. She has over 5 years of experience painting faces and entertaining guests, making her friendly and personable.

Some of her favourite projects to work on are:

  • Face painting & body art
  • Visual merchandising
  • Branding, identity & logo design
  • Marketing print & layout design
  • Illustration & paint design
