Meet Erin

A life long artist, Erin has studied traditional art techniques and graphic design production at Niagara College. She has over 10 years of experience painting faces and entertaining guests, making her friendly and personable.

Some of her favourite projects to work on are:

  • Face painting & body art
  • Visual merchandising
  • Branding, identity & logo design
  • Marketing print & layout design
  • Acrylic painting 

Currently, Erin works full-time in the Retail Marketing industry. She pursues her face painting hobby on evenings, weekends & holidays as her schedule permits. Please contact us to inquire about availability.
