One-Stroke Designs

These artful designs are quick to paint and well suited to entertaining large volumes of guests at events like fundraisers, festivals, school fun fairs, sporting events and corporate parties. Small cheek or arm designs can be completed in 2-3 minutes, averaging 20-30 faces per hour.

    Full Face Designs

    All designs are painted by hand using professional cosmetic-grade face paint, glitter, brushes, sponges and stencils. Full face designs can be completed in 3-10 minutes (depending on the complexity), averaging 8-12 faces per hour. These detailed designs bring the imagination to life and are a great choice for private events like birthday parties, family functions or special occasions.
