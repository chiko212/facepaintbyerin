For more information about rates and booking availability, please contact us by email at esauder@live.com. We look forward to hearing from you!

Face painting is a great addition to any event:

  • company picnics
  • family festivals
  • school fun-fairs
  • grand openings
  • corporate events
  • holiday parties
  • special events and more!

Based out of Oakville, services are available to the Halton, Hamilton, Greater Toronto Area and Niagara Regions. Minimum 3 hour booking required for events outside of the Halton Region. Additional travel charges may apply.
